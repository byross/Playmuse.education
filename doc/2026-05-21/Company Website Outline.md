## **About Us**

### **Our Mission**

Our mission is to make learning more engaging, accessible, and enjoyable through carefully designed digital experiences that respect how children learn and how families live.

### **Our Approach**

We believe effective learning should be:

* Play-based and curiosity-driven  
* Flexible enough to fit into modern family life  
* Designed with children, parents, and educators in mind

### **Our Background**

PlayMuse Education Limited was founded by a parent inspired by her young child’s natural love for music.

Through observing how children engage with sound, rhythm, and creativity in everyday life, the founder began exploring how digital tools could gently support joyful and meaningful learning at home.

Drawing from experience across media, education, and business, PlayMuse was formed to bring together creativity, learning, and technology in a way that feels approachable, flexible, and family-friendly.

## **What We’re Building （Time Stamp)**

### **PlayMuse — A Digital Music Learning Initiative**

PlayMuse is an early-stage digital learning project exploring playful and interactive approaches to music education.

The initiative focuses on supporting foundational musical understanding, including listening, rhythm, and early music concepts, through digital experiences designed for young learners and family-supported learning environments.

The project is currently under development. Further details will be shared at a later stage.
